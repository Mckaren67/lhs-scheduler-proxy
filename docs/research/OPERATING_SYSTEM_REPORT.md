# Residential Cleaning Company Operating System — Research Report

## Document Reviewed

## Key Findings

### Schedule Freeze
- 5pm schedule freeze confirmed as best default
- Day-before notification by 5pm — no penalty
- Morning-of changes — quarterly bonus paused
- Aligns with LHS two-tier system design

### Structured Hiring Funnel
- Knockout questions eliminate unqualified immediately
- Phone screen with scorecard — objective not subjective
- Realistic job preview — reduces first-week turnover
- Paid work sample — 2 hours, real conditions
- Structured panel interview — Karen plus Michael
- No hire below 3 on reliability or safety questions
- No hire without passing paid work sample

### Interview Scorecard
- 5 questions rated 1-5 each
- Maximum score: 25
- Below 15: Decline
- 15-20: Hold for review
- 20-25: Advance to final interview
- Hard stops: reliability below 3, safety below 3

### 90-Day Onboarding
- Onboarding is a system not a single day event
- Day 1: Orientation and shadow
- Week 1: Supervised jobs with mentor
- Week 2-4: Solo jobs with daily check-ins
- Month 2: Reduced check-ins, quality audits
- Month 3: Full independence, performance review
- Each stage has clear pass/fail criteria

### Three Dashboards
1. Executive dashboard — revenue, capacity, retention
2. Operations dashboard — daily schedule, sick days, quality
3. Hiring dashboard — pipeline, time-to-hire, retention rate

### Weekly Cadence
- Monday: Team huddle — week priorities
- Midweek: Quality control checks
- Thursday: Capacity review — next week staffing
- Friday: Weekly scorecard — KPIs reviewed

### Recommended Org Chart
Owner (Michael)
  → Operations Manager (Karen)
    → Scheduler / Client Care
    → Quality / Training Lead
      → Team Leads (2 residential daytime)
        → Cleaning Technicians (15-20)
