# Equipment Emergency SOP

## Trigger
Cleaner texts or calls about broken, forgotten or missing equipment before or during a job.

## Step 1 — Aria asks cleaner
"What equipment is the issue — vacuum, mop, cleaning kit? Do you have any backup?"

## Step 2 — Aria checks
- Is there a backup vacuum at the office?
- Which cleaners are nearby and not yet working?
- How long until the job starts?

## Step 3 — Aria texts Karen immediately
"Equipment emergency — [Cleaner name] — [equipment issue] — [job name] at [time]. Reply YES to arrange backup or NO to handle"

## Step 4 — If Karen replies YES
- Aria coordinates nearest available resource
- Texts affected cleaner with the plan
- Updates job notes in HCP if possible

## Step 5 — Aria logs
- Equipment issue in cleaner profile
- Time Karen spent vs future prevention
- Resolution method for future reference

## Prevention
Sunday evening equipment check SMS sent to all active cleaners at 6pm Pacific.
